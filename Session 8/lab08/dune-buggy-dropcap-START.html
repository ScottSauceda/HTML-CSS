Dune Buggies

Volkswagen's classic Beetle provided its chasis -- as well as its nickname -- to many early dune buggies. Also called beach buggies, these vehicles with extra-wide tires and reinforced suspensions are made to traverse deserts, beaches and other sandy terrain.
